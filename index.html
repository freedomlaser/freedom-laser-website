FAQ

Does the treatment hurt?

No. The session is designed to be painless and non-invasive.

How long does it take?

Plan for about 45 minutes for intake and the session.

Can the treatment help me quit chewing tobacco?

Yes. The approach is used for both smoking and chewing tobacco.

Can it help with appetite suppression?

Yes – Many clients ask about appetite support while changing routines.

Many people start with a single session. If you’d like additional support, follow-up sessions can be discussed.
