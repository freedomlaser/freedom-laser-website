About

Freedom Laser provides low-level laser therapy (LLLT) sessions applied to points commonly used in acupuncture. The goal is to support the body’s natural endorphin response and help reduce cravings and withdrawal discomfort as you quit nicotine.

Many people choose this approach because it’s painless and non-invasive, and appointments are quick. We’ll talk through your goals, your current habits, and what support looks like after your visit.

We also offer appetite suppression support for clients who want help managing hunger while they change routines. Ask about options when you book.

FAQ

Does the treatment hurt?

No. The session is designed to be painless and non-invasive.

How long does it take?

Plan for about 45 minutes for intake and the session.

Can the treatment help me quit chewing tobacco?

Yes. The approach is used for both smoking and chewing tobacco.

Can it help with appetite suppression?

Many clients ask about appetite support while changing routines. Ask us about options when you book.

How many sessions do I need?

Many people start with a single session. If you’d like additional support, follow-up sessions can be discussed.

What is the success rate?

Success varies person to person. Programs using this method often report results around 65% at the 1-year mark.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes. Contact us and tell us your group size for pricing details.

What are your hours and what payment methods do you accept?

We operate by appointment only and can often accommodate weeknights and weekends. We accept cash and credit card.
